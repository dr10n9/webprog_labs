Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper
Preparation Time: 20 min
Calories: 500 kcal

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the garlic and saut√© briefly.
  3. Beat the eggs with grated Parmesan, salt and pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti and mix with the pancetta and egg mixture. Serve immediately.
